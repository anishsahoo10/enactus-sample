About Enactus JU

We are a community of student, academic and business leaders committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to create a more sustainable world.

Our Journey

Enactus Jain University has been at the forefront of creating positive social impact since our inception. We believe in the transformative power of entrepreneurial thinking and action to address the world's most pressing challenges.

Through innovative projects, community engagement, and sustainable business solutions, we're shaping the next generation of social entrepreneurs and changemakers.

Enactus

Our Vision

To create a world where all people have the opportunity to improve their livelihoods through entrepreneurial action.

Our Mission

To engage the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders to use innovation and business principles to improve the world.
